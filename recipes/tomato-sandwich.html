TOMATO SANDWICH

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Fresh Tomato
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Optional: Cheese

Steps

  1. Toast the the bread.
  2. Cut the tomato horizontal. For the thickness, follow your heart desire.
  3. Spread mayonnaise on top of the bread.
  4. Stack the tomatoes neatly.
