DEVIL'S EGG SANDWICHES

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 Whole Eggs
  • 2 Slices of Breads
  • Mayonnaise
  • Optional: Smoke Paprika

Steps

  1. Boil egg in boiling water for 12 minutes.
  2. Toast the bread.
  3. Cool the hard-boiled egg in an ice bath, and peel it once cool
  4. Separate the whites and yolks of the eggs. Then, mix the add paprika, mayonnaise and egg yolk together.
  5. Smash the whites with a fork, and mix the creamed yolk
  6. Spread the mixed eggs together, and add additional vegetables to top of the sandwiches.
