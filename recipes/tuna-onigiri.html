TUNA ONIGIRI

Description

Ingredients

  • Short Grain Rice
  • Tuna
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Optional: Seaweed

Steps

  1. Cook the rice, with 2 to 1 ratio.
  2. Drain the tuna from the can, add mayonnaise, add herbs.
  3. Prepare a bowl of water to wet your hand.
  4. Once the rice is cooked, fluff the rice with cutting motion. Try not to break the rice grain.
  5. Put the tuna filling into the rice and shaped the onigiri with a wet hand.
